Our Story

Nestled in the charming streets of historic Paris, Au Vieux Paris has been a cherished destination for discerning diners since 1923. Our restaurant embodies the elegance and refinement of the Belle Époque era, with its original Belle Époque decor and timeless ambiance.

Every detail, from our Haussmann-era architecture to our carefully curated menu, reflects our commitment to preserving the authentic spirit of old Parisian cuisine and hospitality.

Our experienced team of chefs honors traditional French techniques while celebrating the finest seasonal ingredients from local suppliers.
